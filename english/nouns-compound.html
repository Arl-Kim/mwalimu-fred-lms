Compound Nouns

A compound noun is a noun that is made up of two or more words. The words that form compound nouns may be joined together, separated, or hyphenated.

Examples:

  • Joined:
  • bookcase
  • blackboard
  • pushcart
  • Separated:
  • high school
  • rabbit hutch
  • radar gun
  • Hyphenated:
  • go-getter
  • mother-in-law
  • sergeant-at-arms
