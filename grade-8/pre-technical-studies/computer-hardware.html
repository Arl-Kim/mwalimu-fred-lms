Computer Hardware

Computer hardware is the physical parts of the computer that are tangible. The physical parts of the computer make up the computer hardware.

Computer hardware is made up of the following categories:

  • Central processing unit.

  • Input devices that are also sub-categorized into
    • keying,
    • pointing,
    • scanning,
    • voice input,
    • touch screen,
    • digitizer,
    • digital cameras.

  • Output devices sub-categorized into
    • soft-copy output devices
    • hard-copy output devices.

  • Storage devices sub-categorized into
    • fixed and
    • removable devices.

Categories of computer hardware

The computer hardware is grouped into the following categories:

  • Input devices.

  • Output devices.
