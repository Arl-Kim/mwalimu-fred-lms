Covenants in Modern Life and Their Importance
Examples of Covenants in Modern Life:
  • Land buying contracts.
  • Contracts on employment.
  • Agreements signed by learners.
  • Oath of loyalty.
  • Marriage.
  • Baptism.

Importance of Covenants in Modern Life
  • They promote peace, especially when signed between two fighting communities or nations.
  • They show duties and commitments of the people or parties involved.
  • They enhance economic growth, for example, in cases of business agreements.
  • Covenants promote friendship formation and extend social networks.
  • They promote order and harmony among people involved.
  • They promote integrity in leaders.
  • They enhance diligence at work, for example, reminding people they will be evaluated according to the contracts they signed.
