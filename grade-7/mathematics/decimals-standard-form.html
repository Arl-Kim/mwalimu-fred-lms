Standard Form

A number is said to be in standard form if it is expressed in the form A × 10n, where 1 ≤ A < 10 and n is an integer.

Example

Write the following numbers in standard form:

a. 36

b. 576

c. 0.052

Solution

a. 36/10 × 10 = 3.6 × 101

b. 576/100 × 100 = 5.76 × 102

c. 0.052 = 0.052 × 100/100 = 5.2 × 1/100 = 5.2 × 10-2
