Dear Student, Welcome to Grade 8 Social Studies Week Three

In this week, we shall cover three topics namely:

  • Personality Tests
  • Scientific Theory about Human Origin
  • Importance of Fossil Records of Humans

By the end of this week, you should be able to:

  • Analyze various tools used to carry out personality tests
  • Apply personality test tools for understanding self and others
  • Appreciate personality tests in understanding self and others
  • Explore the scientific theory about human origin
  • Draw charts showing different stages of human evolution
  • Desire to learn more about the scientific theory
  • Explain the importance of fossil records of human evolution in Africa
  • Use IT devices to find more about the importance of fossils
  • Recognize Africa as the cradle of humanity
