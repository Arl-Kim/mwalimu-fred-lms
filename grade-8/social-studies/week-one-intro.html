Welcome to Social Studies Grade 8 Week One

By the end of this week, you should be able to:

  1. Recap the importance of Social Studies.
  2. Describe personal life goals in relation to the choice of careers in Social Studies for self-motivation.
  3. Apply different ways of self-improvement for the attainment of Social Studies career life goals, display values for the achievement of Social Studies life goals.
  4. Explain factors that influence self-esteem in day-to-day life.
  5. Explore ways of building a healthy self-esteem for promotion of social cohesion.
  6. Exhibit healthy self-esteem in social interactions.
  7. Embrace values that enhance healthy self-esteem in day-to-day life.
  8. Describe ways of dealing with difficult emotions in daily life.
  9. Explore real-life situations that require emotional intelligence in day-to-day interactions.
  10. Exhibit emotional intelligence in real-life scenarios.
  11. Appreciate emotional intelligence in real-life situations.
  12. Describe sociopolitical challenges associated with stress management in life.
  13. Exhibit positive ways of managing stress in different situations.
  14. Apply values necessary for managing stress positively in life.
